Cancellation Policy & Refunds

Our cancellation policy is based on the type of booking (Tour Package, Flight, or Hotel) and the proximity to the departure date. **Standard cancellation fees apply** to cover non-refundable deposits made to our vendors.

31+ Days Out

Full refund, minus a small administrative fee (5-10% of total tour cost).

15-30 Days Out

50% of the total cost is non-refundable, reflecting vendor commitments.

Less than 14 Days

100% of the total cost is non-refundable. We recommend travel insurance.

*Note: Specific airlines, non-refundable hotel rooms, and visa fees have separate policies defined at the time of booking. Always check your booking confirmation for details.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I manage or modify an existing booking?

+

You can manage most aspects of your trip, including date changes and traveler details, through the "Your Bookings" link in the header. For major changes to package tours, please contact our 24/7 support line with your booking reference number. Note that modifications may incur fees depending on the supplier's policy.

What is included in a typical 'All-Inclusive' package?

+

Our all-inclusive packages typically cover flights, accommodation, all meals (breakfast, lunch, and dinner), and local transportation as specified in the itinerary. They often include select activities and drinks. Always check the "Inclusions" section on the package detail page to confirm exactly what is covered, as offerings vary by destination and vendor.

Do I need a visa or travel insurance for my destination?

+

Visa requirements depend on your passport's nationality and your destination country. We highly recommend visiting our **Visa Services** page for guidance. While travel insurance is often optional, we strongly advise purchasing it to protect against medical emergencies, cancellations, and lost baggage. You can compare plans on our **Travel Insurance** page.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), net banking, and various UPI/digital wallet options for your convenience. All transactions are secured using industry-standard encryption.

Essential Travel Guidance

Smart Packing Tips

Always pack a small medical kit and a change of clothes in your carry-on. Use packing cubes to maximize space and organization. Remember adapters!

Currency & Budgeting

Inform your bank of your travel dates to avoid card freezing. Use local ATMs for the best exchange rates, and carry small amounts of local cash for markets.

Safety on the Road

Share your itinerary with a family member. Always use reputable, licensed taxis or ride-share services. Keep copies of important documents separate from the originals.
